Flight

Syndrome Incorporated's flight collection is unique in both design and function. Each plane is built for a specific purpose, yet customized for their individual owners. SyInc will build this plane for you and your needs. Find the plane that's right for you.

Viper

Viper is a dual-engine drone that uses titanium rotating blades to hover, and dynamically maneuver using a 9 point sensory system. It is great for high altitude flight.

Velocipod

Velocipod was named for the ferocious spinning blades that line it. The blades help maneuverability in small, tight spaces with high wind pressure. Velocipod can hover close to the ground, water, and even through dense brush effortlessly.

Manta

Manta is made for large groups. It can hold several thousand pounds in machinery, and comes fully installed with loading ports, wire pads, and a customizable security system.
