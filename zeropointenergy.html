Zero Point Energy Gauntlet

The Zero-Point Energy Gauntlet is the first of it's kind in quantum mechaical lazer guidance technology. It's is slated to bring in a new era in law enforcement, construction, and trade.
Using cold fission to contain and block electromyis within the muscle itself, and then levering the subject against the liquineurals siphoned from the atmosphere into the gauntlet, the subject can be safely contained and maneuvered without absolutely no harm to themselves or to others.
Zero-point energy was coined by Buddy Pine during his years at Strigam Technical University. It started as a mere seedling of an idea, before being solidified in his senior year thesis paper. It won accolades including the Bell Phonograph Award for Excellence in Scientific Measures.
It was accompanied by an actual working prototype. Though it was only enough to lift a mouse, now it able to easily lift over two tons. There are no limits for the Zero-Point Energy Gauntlet. Imagine the possibilities for architecture, for zoology, for hospitals, for rescue missions in the Tundra or shipwrecks in the middle of the ocean.

Super strength is no longer just for supers.
