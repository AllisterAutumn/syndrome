The Volcano

Probably the most recognizable landmark that's ever come out of SyInc is 'The Volcano'. It was painstakingly drilled and furnished with SyInc's patented heat deflecting glass panels to be able to sustain human life. There is nothing more emboldening, awe-inspiring, and humbling than being in an actual live volcano.
It is located on the far end of Nomanison Island, equipped with it's own facilities and machines.
The volcano was necessary for it's heat properties, and the unique materials only found on Nomanison island. Instead of building tunnels and tubes to siphon the lava, innovators at SyInc designed ways for people to actually inhabit the volcano, and it is truly inspiring to behold.
Unfortunately, the volcano is not open to guest visits and is only accesible to cleared personnel, because of the nature of the equipment it holds.
