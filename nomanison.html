Nomanison Island

Far into the Atlantic Ocean lies Nomanison Island, untouched by anything but the elements. The perfect setting for a high-tech state of the art laboratory that uses green sources of energy. Water mills, hydropumps, solar panels, wind mills, steam energy. Neutrino labs, vacuum tunnels, The Observatory. We have it all.
Gaining Nomanison Island was truly one of the landmark moments in SyInc history. If Metroville has our roots, then Nomanison Island is where we are truly allowed to fluorish. Many of our team has relocated to the island. We've built fully functioning dormatories with all amenities. We also have weekly flights to and from the island, and are open every weekend for visits and tours.
Just don't get too comfortable, or you might not want to come back!
