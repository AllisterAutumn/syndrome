Metroville

When Syndrome Incorporated was first started twenty years ago in 9 year old Buddy Pine's basement, it was truly the beginning of something amazing. That's why, when the company bloomed into the formidable company it is today, we knew that we had to have a location here. It is important to remember where one comes from, and it is even more essential to give back to the community that fostered you. The Metroville SyInc location has many programs aimed at students, the adults, the disabled, and others. We have funded thousands of dollars in scholarship programs and will continue to promote growth and progress in Metroville.
