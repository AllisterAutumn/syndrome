Opening of Liminal Laboratories
4.8.18

On 4.8.18 Buddy Pine, CEO and Founder of Syndrome Industries (SyInc) cut the ribbon that marked the official opening of Liminal Laboratories.

Liminal Lab was the brain-child of Buddy Pine and renowned geneticist Dr. Christine Tatum to devote an entire five floor building to studying the many variations and mutations possible in the human genome. The goal of the lab is to create a state-of-the-art space where innovation, science, and discovery is at the goal of every project.
Like SyInc, Liminal Labs is privately owned, but will still do its best to give back to the community. A second branch of the Buddy Pine S.W.A.N program will be opened, as will a $50,000 Scholarship for young people heading intp the biology field.
The celebration was continued with speeches from Mayor Judith Reed and Senator McCraig. Both espoused the benefits that Liminal Labs would bring to the community. Afterwards, guests headed inside for the banquet and evening gala where they were treated with celebrity performances from Odile, The Glass Trio, and others.

It was truly a momentous occasion having Dr. Christine Tatum take control of a dream that has been so long in the making. Liminal Laboratories is set to be the leading genetics research lab in the world with a solid set of scientists and brilliant minds at the hiring roster. Dr. Tatum's research will set the groundwork for discovering breakthroughs in the mysteries of the human body.

Liminal Laboratories was made possible through gracious donations from SyInc, the parent corporation which also donates to many other foundations dedicatd to the enrichment of the human race.

Syndrome Inc. fully supports the ideas that Liminal Labs was built on. We look forward to working together to finding the cures to the many dangerous mutations possible in the human genome.
